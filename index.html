About Banksy

An activist known for his graffiti, Banksy places his marks around the world and has been doing so since the 1990s. Using lifelike details, Banksy poignantly chooses areas for their political, social, or cultural conflicts as part of his artistic method. Known for its picture-like quality, Banksy’s stencil art deviates from popular graffiti.

As to Banksy’s true identity, it currently remains unknown. He could be an individual, a group, or an idea. What is more important than his identity is his influence; especially regarding social movements.

Stencil art (pochoir) - Banksy’s signature style. A cut out is used to maintain sharp, concise edges, and is typically reusable.
